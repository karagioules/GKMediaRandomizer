🚀 HOW TO USE:

1. Double-click PriveRandomizer.exe on your desktop
2. Click "📁 Select Folder" to choose media location
3. App scans folder recursively for images & videos
4. Navigate with arrow keys (← →) or spacebar
5. Click "🗑️ Delete" to remove unwanted files

Keyboard Controls:
  → or Space  =  Next image/video
  ←           =  Previous image/video
